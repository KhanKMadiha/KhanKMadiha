## Projects

### Articulate

A daily reading practice app for professionals who want to speak with more confidence. Claude generates a personalised passage based on your job title, goals, and speaking style. You record yourself and get scored on accuracy, fluency, and pace. Includes a dyslexia-friendly mode.

**Stack:** React, Node.js, Express, Anthropic Claude API, Web Speech API, Railway, Docker

[Live app](https://articulate-app-production.up.railway.app) · [Repo](https://github.com/KhanKMadiha/articulate-app)
